# Past Simple
We use this time when something happened and finished
**Example**: I got in my car

# Past Continuos
We use this when we need to say something in past but the actions are happening in that past moment. Describe a scene or situation, that continue for some time.

**Example**: The sun was shining yesterday, At 8 pm, Lee **was** still **sleeping**, The dog **was barking** loudly

# Past perfect
We use this when something happened but we don't know the time what it happened or if still happening
**Example**: It had been raining and **all my washing had got wet**

## Feedback
La Regla de Oro para tu Cerebro

Imagina una línea de tiempo.
1. **Past Perfect (Had + Participio):** Es el **Flashback**. Lo que ocurrió _antes_ de lo que estás contando.
    - _Ej: El servidor **había caído** (had crashed)._
2. **Past Simple (Verbo en pasado):** Es la **Historia Principal**.
    - _Ej: Yo **reinicié** (restarted) el servicio._
**Frase para tatuarse:** _"Cuando llegué (**Simple**), el problema ya había empezado (**Perfect**)."_
